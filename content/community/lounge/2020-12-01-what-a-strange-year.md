Hopefully, next year we will be able to get back into the ring and even more importantly meet up with our friends. 

As a dog groomer as well as showing I have tried hundreds of different products from cheap to mega expensive. Sadly, I have not found one shampoo that suits all. In my experience mixing Shampoo’s, conditioners, conditioning sprays from different brands works the best. 

Please do not use human shampoos, conditioners on your dogs they have chemicals in them that can been harmful to your dogs.

Here is my Top Shampoos and conditioners

•	Superfine Ultimate show in my opinion this shampoo is does what it says on the tin and   	is a really good price, Their super silk conditioner smells amazing and works well.

•	My next choice is a bit pricier but to be honest has worked its magic on most coats Chris Christensen Spectrum 10.

•	Artero shampoos and conditioners are another great choice their moisturizing and Detox shampoos are fab.

•	For those of you that like natural shampoos and conditioners for me there are only 2.    

Melanie Newman’s the Purify is amazing on white or light coats.

•	Or another great one is For All Dog Kind.

•	My favourite grooming spray is Mix by Artero.

•	Brushes and combs, are I think are a very personal thing this can depend on all sorts of different things anything from thickness / thinness of coats to if you have any ailments such as carpel tunnel arthritis etc.

•	Here are some of my favourites I personally don’t think you can go wrong with any Chriss Christian brushes yes they are pricey but worth it, anything from the Big G to their pin brushes .

•	As for combs I don’t think you can go wrong with a greyhound comb. As they give you narrow and wide teeth in one comb.

•	But my personal favourite is the Utsumi combs they are strong but so light weight.

I hope you find some of these helpful happy grooming.

Joanne Beddoe, Aspera
