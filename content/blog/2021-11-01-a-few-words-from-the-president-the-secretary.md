Well the show world tentatively started to come back to life half way through the year... its committees working their way through all the red tape which had to be adhered to due to the covid restrictions.

Dates of shows were altered and the Kennel Club allowed societies to hold two Championship shows instead of one due to shows being cancelled last year, which NOELAC took advantage of and we went ahead and held our cancelled November 2020 show in August this year. It felt so good to be back and socialising again. Our second show went ahead as usual in November and once again proved to be a great success.

Hopefully, fingers crossed, this is the start of getting things back to normal so we can begin to organise other events for our members.

We hope you all enjoy reading our latest newsletter and that we will see you all at one of our events next year.

Christmas and New Year is creeping up fast, time to reflect on the past couple of years and take stock of what really matters... life is short so live it, make the best of it and remember what really matters... family and friends, good times and love.

A big **THANK YOU** to all our members for supporting the club.
