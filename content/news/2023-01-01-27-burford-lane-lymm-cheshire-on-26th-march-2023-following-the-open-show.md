> 27, Burford Lane, Lymm, Cheshire 
> On 26th March 2023 Following the Open show

Any member wishing an item/items to be placed on the agenda must ensure that written notification of such is in the hands of the secretary no later than 10th March 2023.

All items for inclusion on the agenda, including nominations for officers, committee and show judges can only be accepted if duly nominated and seconded in writing by fully paid up members.

Nominations will be accepted by post or email (rule 4.5)

Only one person from a joint nomination to propose or second the same nomination (rule 4.6)

Nominees for Officers and committee must have been a club member for at least two years (rule 4.1)

Nominees for Officers, committee and judges MUST indicate in writing that they are willing and able to accept the nomination, (rule 4.3/15.5)
